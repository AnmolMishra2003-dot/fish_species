About AquaID

AquaID is built as part of a B.Tech Computer Science project at Babu Banarasi Das Institute of Technology & Management, Lucknow. It addresses the critical need for accurate, species-level catch reporting in India's fisheries sector, empowering fishermen with AI-powered identification, GPS geotagging, and real-time weather data.
